Effect of SNR Degradation in DNN Object Detection Models
This research project focuses on understanding how signal-to-noise ratio (SNR) impacts the performance of deep neural networks in object detection tasks. We simulate degradation in image quality and evaluate changes in accuracy and detection reliability across different object detection models. The insights from this study are intended to improve robustness of AI systems in noisy or low-quality visual environments, such as satellite imagery or low-light scenarios.
